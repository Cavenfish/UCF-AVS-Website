Webinar

Astrochemistry Webinar Series

  • Virtual (Zoom)
  • May-June 2020

The UCF AVS student chapter is organizing an online, virtual Webinar series on topics in theoretical and experimental astrochemistry, an exciting and highly interdisciplinary field. We are currently seeking professionals to give lectures on their astrochemical research.

Although the event was originally intended to be a day-long short course event on UCF's campus, due to COVID-19, we have decided to make the event a virtual lecture series throughout the months of May and June. More news is coming soon!

To register for the short course, please fill out this Google form.

If you are interested in contributing a lecture to the series, please email AVSknights@gmail.com.

Organizers

Brian Ferrari
Katie Slavicinska
Brian is a Physics PhD candidate at the University of Central Florida working for Dr. Christopher Bennett. His research interests include UV and electron irradiation of astrophysically relevant ices and quantum chemistry calculations of spectroscopic properties of gas-phase molecules. Katie is a Planetary Science PhD candidate at the University of Central Florida working for Dr. Christopher Bennett. Her research interests include origins of life and astrobiology, meteoritics, and chemical evolution in the cosmic environment.
